Package of Practice

Complete cultivation guide for maximum yield and quality production

Land Preparation

  • Deep plowing during summer months
  • 2-3 cross harrowings for fine tilth
  • Level the field properly for uniform water distribution
  • Apply FYM @ 10-12 tonnes per hectare during last plowing

Sowing Time

  • Optimal: 15th October to 15th November
  • Late sowing: Up to 30th November (with reduced yield)
  • Avoid sowing after December 1st
  • Early sowing may lead to pest problems

Seed Treatment

  • Treat seeds with Carbendazim @ 2g/kg seed
  • Inoculate with Rhizobium culture @ 10g/kg seed
  • Use PSB culture @ 10g/kg seed for better phosphorus uptake
  • Air dry treated seeds in shade before sowing

Sowing Method

  • Method: Line sowing with seed drill
  • Row spacing: 30 cm
  • Seed depth: 3-4 cm
  • Seed rate: 75-80 kg per hectare

Fertilizer Management

  • Nitrogen: 20-25 kg/ha
  • Phosphorus: 40-50 kg/ha
  • Potash: 20 kg/ha
  • Apply full dose of P & K and half N as basal
  • Remaining N after 30-35 days of sowing

Irrigation Management

  • Pre-sowing: Light irrigation if soil moisture is insufficient
  • 1st irrigation: 35-40 days after sowing (branching stage)
  • 2nd irrigation: 60-65 days after sowing (flowering stage)
  • 3rd irrigation: 80-85 days after sowing (pod filling stage)

Cultivation Timeline

Days 0-15: Land Preparation & Sowing

Complete land preparation, seed treatment, and sowing operations

Days 15-30: Germination & Early Growth

Monitor germination, apply post-emergence herbicides if needed

Days 30-45: Vegetative Growth

First irrigation, top dressing of nitrogen, intercultural operations

Days 45-65: Branching & Flowering

Second irrigation, pest monitoring, foliar sprays if required

Days 65-85: Pod Formation & Filling

Third irrigation, disease management, avoid water stress

Days 85-105: Maturity & Harvesting

Monitor maturity signs, harvest at proper time, post-harvest handling

Pest & Disease Management

Integrated approach for healthy crop production

Major Pests

Pod Borer Chlorpyriphos 20 EC @ 2 ml/liter
Aphids Imidacloprid 17.8 SL @ 0.5 ml/liter
Thrips Fipronil 5% SC @ 2 ml/liter
Leaf Miner Cyromazine 75 WP @ 0.5 g/liter

Major Diseases

Wilt Seed treatment + soil drenching with Carbendazim
Root Rot Trichoderma viride @ 5g/kg seed
Blight Mancozeb 75% WP @ 2.5g/liter
Rust Propiconazole 25% EC @ 1ml/liter

Organic Alternatives

  • Neem oil @ 5ml/liter for pest control
  • Trichoderma culture for soil-borne diseases
  • Pseudomonas fluorescens for bacterial diseases
  • Panchagavya spray @ 3% for plant vigor
  • Trap crops like marigold for pest management
  • Balanced nutrition for disease resistance
